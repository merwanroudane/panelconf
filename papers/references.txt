 COPYRIGHT NOTE
 --------------
 The paper PDFs are NOT redistributed in this repository. Almost all are
 published by Elsevier, Wiley, Taylor & Francis or Oxford University Press,
 or distributed via JSTOR, and are protected by copyright (JSTOR's terms
 prohibit redistribution explicitly). Use the DOI links below - they always
 resolve to the publisher's official version.

 The one PDF included, Karavias_Tzavalis_Zhang_2022_Econometrics.pdf, is
 open access under a CC BY licence.

 DOIs marked [OK] were read directly from the papers themselves.
 Entries without a DOI give the full citation so the paper is findable.

 Please cite the original methodological papers when using the xt* commands.
